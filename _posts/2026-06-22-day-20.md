what_i_learned: |
Today started with a session where we learned how to fill out our timesheets correctly. We were guided through the process of documenting our work hours accurately and learned about the importance of maintaining proper records throughout the internship. Understanding how to complete timesheets correctly is an essential professional skill and will help ensure that our work is properly documented.

Afterward, we had a brief introduction to Overleaf. During this session, we explored some of the platform's basic features and learned how it can be used for collaborative academic writing. Since our research project involves preparing a research paper, becoming familiar with Overleaf will help us organize our work more efficiently and collaborate more effectively with team members.

Later in the day, we participated in a team game activity. The game was both fun and engaging, encouraging teamwork, communication, and problem-solving. Although our team did not win, the experience was enjoyable and provided an opportunity to interact with others outside of our usual research activities. The activity reminded us of the importance of collaboration and maintaining a positive attitude when facing challenges.

Following the game, we returned to the research lab and continued working on our research paper. We reviewed our progress, discussed ideas with teammates, and worked on organizing different sections of the paper. Continuing to develop the paper helped us better understand the research process and the importance of clearly communicating our findings and objectives.

reflection: |
Today was a productive day that combined professional development, teamwork, and research. Learning how to complete timesheets correctly may seem like a small task, but it highlighted the importance of responsibility and accurate record-keeping in professional environments. These skills will be valuable not only during this internship but also in future careers.

The introduction to Overleaf was also beneficial because it exposed us to a tool commonly used in academic and research settings. Although I am still learning how to navigate the platform, I can already see how useful it will be when collaborating on research documents and managing large writing projects.

The team-building game was one of the most enjoyable parts of the day. Even though we lost, the experience was fun and allowed us to strengthen our communication and teamwork skills. Returning to the lab afterward and continuing work on our research paper helped reinforce the importance of balancing collaboration, learning, and productivity throughout the research process.

Another challenge was organizing content for the research paper and deciding how to structure different sections. Academic writing requires careful planning and attention to detail, which can sometimes be difficult when working with a large amount of information.
