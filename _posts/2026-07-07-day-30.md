what_i_learned: |
  Today I trained our baseline 1D-CNN model using the PTB-XL ECG dataset.
  I learned how the model processes ECG signals, calculates prediction errors,
  and updates its weights through backpropagation over multiple training epochs.
  I monitored the training and validation loss after each epoch to evaluate the
  model's performance and observed how the loss generally decreased as training
  progressed. After completing the training, I saved the trained model so it can
  be reused without retraining. I also explored the fairness evaluation phase,
  where we analyzed the model's False Negative Rates across different age and
  sex demographic groups to identify whether any group experienced more missed
  diagnoses than others.
