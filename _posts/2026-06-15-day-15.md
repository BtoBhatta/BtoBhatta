what_i_learned: |
Today was our first day meeting in person, and it was a fun and exciting experience. We started the day with an orientation session where we learned more about the program, upcoming activities, and expectations. Everyone introduced themselves, which helped us get to know each other better and build connections within the group.

We also discussed presentations and learned about effective communication skills that will help us present our projects in the future. Listening to different perspectives and ideas gave me a better understanding of teamwork and collaboration.

During lunch, the organizers provided tacos for everyone. It was a great opportunity to relax, socialize, and continue conversations with other participants outside of the formal sessions.

After lunch, we participated in team-building games. Our team worked together very well, using strong communication and collaboration skills. We were excited to win one of the games, which made the activity even more enjoyable.

Later in the day, we headed to the research room, where we discussed our projects and shared ideas with our teammates and mentors. These discussions helped us better understand our research goals and the next steps for our work.
