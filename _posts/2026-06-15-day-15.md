what_i_learned: |
Today was our first day meeting in person, and it was a very enjoyable and exciting experience. We started the day with an orientation session where we learned more about the program and what to expect in the coming weeks. Everyone introduced themselves, which helped us get to know each other better and build connections within the group.

We also discussed presentations and shared ideas about how we would communicate our research and project progress. This gave us a better understanding of the expectations and helped us prepare for future presentations.

One of the highlights of the day was lunch. The organizers provided tacos, which gave us an opportunity to relax, socialize, and continue getting to know our teammates outside of the formal sessions.

After lunch, we participated in several team-building games. Our team worked together effectively, demonstrating strong communication, collaboration, and problem-solving skills. We were excited to win one of the games, which made the experience even more memorable.

Later in the day, we headed to the research room, where we discussed our ongoing projects and exchanged ideas. These discussions helped us better understand our research goals and encouraged teamwork as we continue developing our work.

reflection: |
Overall, today was a fun and productive first in-person meeting. I enjoyed meeting everyone face-to-face, participating in activities, and working together as a team. The experience strengthened our communication and collaboration skills while also helping us make progress on our research projects. I am looking forward to future in-person sessions and continuing to learn from my peers and mentors.
