After lunch, we participated in team-building games. Our team worked well together, showing strong communication and collaboration skills, and we were excited to win one of the games.

Later, we went to the research room where we discussed our projects and shared ideas with teammates and mentors. These discussions helped us better understand our research goals and next steps.
