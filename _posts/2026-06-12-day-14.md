what_i_learned: |
  Today we had a presentation showcase where each group shared what they worked on throughout the week. Our focus was on the ECG classification project, including the development of the 1D-CNN and Hybrid CNN-Transformer models. It was interesting to see how different teams approached similar problems and how everyone explained their progress in their own way.

  During our presentation, we explained our project idea and described how the 1D-CNN model is used to extract important ECG features from the signal. We also explained how the Hybrid CNN-Transformer model combines CNN layers and Transformer layers to better understand both local and long-range patterns in ECG data. Presenting our work helped me understand our project more clearly and improved my confidence in explaining technical concepts.

  After our presentation, we listened to other groups and learned about their projects. It was interesting to see different applications of machine learning and how AI is being used in different ways. The discussion and feedback from other teams helped us reflect on our own work and think about improvements.

blockers: |
  One challenge was feeling nervous while presenting technical concepts in front of others. It was also sometimes difficult to explain complex ideas like CNN and Transformer models in a simple way.

reflection: |
  Today was a very interactive and interesting day. Presenting our weekly progress helped me understand our project better, and listening to other groups gave me new ideas and perspectives. Overall, it was a good learning experience that improved my communication skills and understanding of machine learning projects.
