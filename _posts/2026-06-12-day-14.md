what_i_learned: |
Today we had a presentation showcase where each group shared what they worked on throughout the week. Our focus was on the ECG classification project, including the development of the 1D-CNN and Hybrid CNN-Transformer models. It was interesting to see how different teams approached similar problems and how everyone explained their progress in their own way.

During our presentation, we discussed the main idea of our project, how the 1D-CNN model works for extracting ECG features, and how the Hybrid CNN-Transformer model combines CNN and Transformer layers for better signal understanding. Presenting our work helped me understand the project more clearly and also improved my confidence in explaining technical concepts.

After our presentation, we listened to other groups and learned about their projects as well. It was very interesting to see different applications of machine learning and how each team is using AI in unique ways. The discussions and feedback sessions helped us reflect on our own work and think about possible improvements.

blockers: |
One challenge was feeling nervous while presenting technical concepts in front of others. Also, it was sometimes difficult to explain complex ideas like CNN and Transformer models in a simple way.

reflection: |
Today was a very interactive and interesting day. Presenting our weekly progress helped me better understand our project, and listening to other groups gave me new ideas and perspectives. Overall, it was a good learning experience that improved both my communication skills and understanding of machine learning projects.
