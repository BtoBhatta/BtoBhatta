what_i_learned: |
  Today was one of the most engaging and interactive days so far in the program. We started with a project discussion where the group talked in detail about our research direction, goals, and how we plan to approach the work moving forward. It was helpful to hear different ideas from teammates and understand how each person is thinking about the project from their own perspective.

  After the discussion, we divided the work among group members so that everyone had specific responsibilities. This made the project feel more organized and manageable, and it also helped me understand the importance of teamwork in research settings. I was assigned a part of the work that required me to explore and contribute actively, which made me feel more involved and responsible.

  Later, we worked on Google Colab, where we started experimenting and running parts of our project. It was a really good hands-on experience because I got to see how code and ideas actually come together in a practical environment. Even though some parts were a little challenging at first, it became easier as we collaborated and helped each other troubleshoot and understand the steps.

  We also participated in a Kahoot activity, which made learning more fun and interactive. It was a nice break from technical work and helped reinforce some of the concepts we have been learning. The competitive but friendly environment made it even more enjoyable.

  In addition, we explored concepts related to generative AI, which I found really interesting. Learning how AI models generate responses and assist in problem-solving made me more curious about how this technology can be applied in real-world scenarios, especially in research and healthcare.

blockers: |
  There were no major blockers today, but I did feel a bit of confusion at certain points while working in Google Colab since some of the steps were new to me. However, working with the group helped me understand things better, and I was able to follow along more comfortably as the session continued.

reflection: |
  Today was honestly very exciting and fun. I really enjoyed the combination of structured project work and interactive activities like Kahoot. It made the learning process feel less stressful and more engaging.

  I also felt more connected to my team today since we worked closely together and divided tasks. It made the project feel real and collaborative rather than individual work.

  Exploring generative AI was definitely a highlight for me because it showed me how powerful and useful these tools are becoming. It made me even more interested in learning more about AI and how it can be used in different fields, especially in healthcare and research.

  Overall, I left today feeling motivated, curious, and excited for what we will build next as a team.
