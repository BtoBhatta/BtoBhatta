what_i_learned: |
  Today we spent time in breakout rooms with our mentors discussing our research project and setting goals for the week ahead. We talked about the progress we have made so far, what tasks need to be completed, and how each team member can contribute to moving the project forward.

  A major focus of today's session was learning about AI model development. We began working on building a 1D Convolutional Neural Network (1D-CNN) using the PTB-XL ECG dataset. Our mentor explained the purpose of the model, how ECG data is processed, and how the neural network learns patterns from ECG signals to make predictions.

  We worked in Google Colab to implement and understand different parts of the model architecture. The mentor walked us through the code step by step, explaining how the layers work together and how data flows through the network. This helped me better understand how machine learning models are built and trained using real healthcare data.

  We also discussed the next steps of the project, including model training, evaluation, and comparing different architectures. These discussions helped me gain a clearer understanding of our overall project goals and how today's work fits into the larger research process.
