what_i_learned: |
Today started with a breakout room session where we discussed Model 2, the Transformer Hybrid model, for our project. Our mentor explained the model in detail and helped us understand how it works. Since I have been assigned the research writing portion of our project, he also showed us examples of research papers and explained the structure and key components of academic writing. This session helped me gain a better understanding of how to organize and present research findings effectively.

Later, we had another literature review session with Dr. Pandey. She explained the concepts very clearly and made the session highly interactive by encouraging questions and discussions. Her explanations helped me better understand the research papers we have been reviewing and how they connect to our project.

After lunch, we participated in a fun activity where ChatGPT generated pictures based on what it knew about each of us. The first image was surprisingly accurate, but I decided to provide a more detailed prompt and generated a second version that reflected me even better. It was interesting to see how AI interpreted personal information and interests through images.

During our breakout room discussion, everyone shared their generated images and talked about how accurately they represented them. It was a fun and engaging way to connect with our peers. We ended the day by continuing our project work, focusing on coding tasks and taking notes related to our project development. Overall, today was a productive balance of research, learning, collaboration, and creativity.
